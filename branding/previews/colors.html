Toolskin · Color System

One accent. Onyx surfaces. HSL engine.

Every color in the system is driven by three custom properties: --ts-accent-h, --ts-accent-s, --ts-accent-l. Change the hue, the entire ramp follows. Surfaces are blue-shifted neutrals — cool, never grey. Dark-first.
