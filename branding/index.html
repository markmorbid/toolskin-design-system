The proof.

Open any demo, view source, search for --ts-accent-h. You'll find one declaration per file. Change it. Reload. The whole product retunes — buttons, glow, status pills, charts, hover states, contrast on accent — without a single component rewrite.

The honest caveat.

The audit is graded B+, not A. Some components still hardcode opacity, light-mode parity isn't done, and motion is mostly hover. Read the scorecard for the full list — and what's coming in v1.2.
